# SCH — Front matter (Vorspann)

OCR transcription, with English and Russian translations, of the **front matter** of:

> **Richard Schmidt**, *Nachträge zum Sanskrit-Wörterbuch in kürzerer Fassung von Otto Böhtlingk* — Leipzig 1928, Verlag von Otto Harrassowitz.

A supplement (*Nachträge*) to the abridged Petersburg dictionary (PWK), part of the [Cologne Digital Sanskrit Lexicon](https://www.sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/) (CDSL). The front matter comprises the **title page** and a four-page **Vorwort** (foreword), signed *Münster (Westphalia), 26 September 1924* by Richard Schmidt.

- **Source language:** German (1928 orthography, preserved verbatim — e.g. *daß, resp., Maßstab*-era spellings).
- **Source scans:** Cologne csldoc, [SCH front-matter index](https://sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/scans/csldev/csldoc/build/dictionaries/prefaces/schpref.html).
- The tiny digitizer running-header and footer stamps that appear on every scan (*"Institute of Indology & Tamil Studies, Cologne University …"*) are **not** part of the original and have been omitted.
- Printed page numbers (Roman, VI–VIII) are kept inline as `[page N]`; the Vorwort opening page (V) carries no printed number.
- Schmidt's own sardonic editorial mark `[? ?]` (questioning the Leipzig "experts") is printed in the original and transcribed as such.
